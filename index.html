About Comikey

Our goal is to bring in a new online reading experience!

We are building a unified platform for all the different kind of content from the Asian markets, Manga - webtoons - manhua.

Our team consists of members who have years of experience translating and editing Asian content.

We plan to launch with 10+ series amazing series of different categories.

Our future goal is to build a relationship with and bring over great works from the Korean and Japanese market.

Readers will be able to read all the content for free with updated free to read new chapters every week, and will have the option to purchase keys to unlock adless experience, customizable features and purchase early access of limited chapters.
